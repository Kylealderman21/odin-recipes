Kyle's World Famous Beef Chile Verde

Description:

Hatch Chiles are perfect for creating a delicious, slow cooked, beef chile verde in the crock pot. This
recipe is perfect for impressing large crowds with minimal work and clean up! Follow the instructions
below for the best, mouth watering beef chile verde.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Hatch Chiles
  • 3 lb Beef Chuck
  • 1 Large Yellow Onion
  • 6 Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 Roma Tomatoes
  • Olive Olive
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat oven to broil or the highest setting on your oven
  2. Wash and cut the stems of the Hatch Chiles and cut the Tomatoes into fourths
  3. Peel your onion and garlic and cut the onion into fourths
  4. Place the Hatch Chiles, Tomatoes, Onion and Garlic onto a baking tray
  5. Generously cover the ingredients on the baking tray with olive oil, salt, and pepper
  6. Once the oven is preheated, roast the ingredients until charred (About 15 - 20 Minutes)
  7. While these are roasting, grab your chuck roast, pat it dry, and season generously with salt and pepper
  8. Heat up a cast iron or pan with olive oil until smoking
  9. Sear the chuck roast on all sides on high heat until you get a nice crust
  10. Place the chuck roast in the crock pot once it has developed a nice crust
  11. Once the vegetables are done roasting in the oven, grab a blender and blend all the ingredients together until smooth
  12. Season to taste with salt and pepper then add the mixture to the crock pot with the chuck roast
  13. Cover and set the crock pot to roast for 8 hours on low and slow
  14. After 8 hours, shred and serve with rice and beans or make tacos and burritos!
